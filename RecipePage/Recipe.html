What You'll Need:

  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose four
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cacao powder
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

What's better than a cookie-brownie hybrid? Nothing, that's what. This is one of my all-time favorite recipes- chocolate crinkle cookies. Not only is it simple and easy to make, but tastes phenomenal. You won't be able to stop at just one, trust me. ;)

So.... let's get into it!

Directions:

  1. Sift flour, cocoa powder, granulated sugar, baking powder and salt into a bowl.
  2. Mix in vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in dry ingredients 1/3 at time until thoroughly combined.
  4. Cover and chill dough for at least an hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  6. Roll 2-3 tablespoons of cookie dough, toss in powdered sugar competely, and place on baking sheet 2 in apart.
  7. Bake for 10-13 minutes until cookies looked 'cracked'.
  8. Cool completely and ENJOY!
